Welcome to SharePlate

An online platform designed to minimize food wastage by connecting donors with NGOs.

Our Mission

SharePlate is dedicated to bridging the gap between generous donors and the NGOs that serve those in need. Our platform facilitates the efficient redistribution of surplus food, ensuring that no meal goes to waste and that it reaches the people who need it most.

How It Works

For Donors

  1. Register & Login: Create your account to get started.
  2. List Your Donation: Provide details about the surplus food you have.
  3. Manage Requests: View and accept requests from NGOs.

For NGOs

  1. Register & Login: Sign up your organization to connect with donors.
  2. Find Nearby Donors: Search for available food donations in your area.
  3. Request & Track: Make requests and track the donation status in real-time.
